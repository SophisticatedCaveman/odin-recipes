Butternut Squash Soup

The Soup

Nothing says winter is coming like a warm bowl of butternut squash soup. This is a recipes for a healthy bowl of soup that'll wake up your taste buds with the natural sweetness of butternut squash.

Ingredients

  • Butternut Squash - 1 medium sized
  • Carrot and Onion - to your liking
  • Apple - just the one
  • Veggie Broth - or chicken broth
  • Coconut milk
  • Seasonings - garlic, fresh sage, cayenne, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, sea salt, and black pepper.

Steps

  1. Cook Sautee onion and garlic in a bit of olive oil. Add in squash and veggies and cook until they are completely tender, then add in coconut milk.
  2. Blend Use immersion blender to puree soup until smooth.
  3. SeasonAdd your preferred seasonings, especially salt and pepper.
  4. Serve Then serve some or store it for the winter ahead.
